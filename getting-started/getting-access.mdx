The Polymarket Exchange API is **not currently open to the public**. Access is restricted and available only through an application process.

## API Access Restrictions

Currently, the Polymarket Exchange API is in a **closed access** phase. This means:
- Trading and market data access requires explicit authorization
- Rate limits and usage terms are determined on a case-by-case basis

## Applying for Access

If you're interested in accessing the Polymarket Exchange API, you can express your interest and apply for access through our application process.

### Application Requirements

When applying, please be prepared to provide:

- **Use Case Description**: Detailed explanation of how you plan to use the API
- **Technical Background**: Information about your development experience and infrastructure
- **Compliance Information**: Details about your regulatory compliance capabilities
- **Business Information**: Company or organization details (if applicable)
- **Expected Usage**: Anticipated request volume and trading patterns

### Application Form

To apply for API access or express interest in future availability, please complete the following form:

[Apply Here](https://forms.gle/ptLusdHnRkXZkvAs5)

## What to Expect

After submitting your application:

1. **Review Process**: Applications are reviewed by our technical and compliance teams
2. **Follow-up**: You may be contacted for additional information or clarification
3. **Decision**: You'll be notified of the decision via email

> **Important**: This page reflects the current status of Polymarket Exchange API access. Policies and availability may change. Please check back regularly for updates or contact our team directly for the most current information.
